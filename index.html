About Our Company

DOKTAIN RESOURCES LTD (DRL) is an indigenous engineering servicing company, registered to provide wide range of Building and Civil Works, Maintenance services, Corrosion control, Marine Construction and Procurement, Security services, procurement of Oil Tools/Industrial materials and General contractor to both public and private sectors of the Nigerian economy

DRL was incorporated as a private business before operating for about ten (10) in Oil and Gas sector with a business name- DOKTAIN RESOURCES LTD (DRL) Our management engineers have all been involved in various engineering and construction projects, and have considerable years of experience.

DRL projects proceed at a pace unmatched by other contracting servicing companies due to our unique way of making sure that our clients achieve prompt delivery of their projects, without compromising with quality and standards. We continue to offer new innovation, cost effective programs that will meet our client satisfaction.This will be made possible by our inhouse engineers, project managers and technicians who are well versed in their different fields. Our quality control is ISO 9001 complaint, and our communities affairs policy meet international standard.
